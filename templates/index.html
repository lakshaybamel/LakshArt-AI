FAQ

Project details at a glance.

What architecture does LakshArt AI use?

The project uses AdaIN (Adaptive Instance Normalization) to transfer style statistics onto content features before decoding the final image.

Why are there a pretrained decoder and VGG encoder?

The VGG encoder extracts deep features from the uploaded images, and the pretrained decoder reconstructs the stylized output quickly from the transformed features.

What datasets were used for training?

COCO Dataset 2017 was used for content representations and Painter by Numbers for artistic style learning.

What powers the web app?

The UI is served by Flask and the image synthesis runs through PyTorch, so the browser interaction stays simple while the model inference happens on the backend.

Can I tune how strong the style looks?

Yes. The Style Strength slider controls the balance between preserving content details and emphasizing artistic style.

About Project

Built as a lightweight AI-powered neural art application.

LakshArt AI is a Flask + PyTorch Neural Style Transfer app focused on a fast, minimal workflow: upload two images, transfer style with AdaIN, and download the generated result.

The interface focuses on simplicity and highlights the core neural style transfer experience.
